About

Who is Sean?

Sean works from home as a web developer, which gives him the perfect chance to walk your dog during the work day.
He's a father of three (including twins), so he has plenty of experience meeting the needs of little ones.
Previous dog experience includes labradors, golden retrievers, corgis, and mixed breeds.

What does Sean do?

Sean walks dogs, and he's excited to lend his services to your special pet.
If you live in the Lake Mary area (especially The Terraces, Pebble Creek, and Canterbury Crossings) then Sean is ready to speak with you about walking your dog.
